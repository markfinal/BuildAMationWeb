Prerequisites

Windows

Windows 7 and 8 are compatible, in both 32-bit and 64-bit flavours.

C/C++ compiler toolchains

In support of free developer tools, VisualStudio Express, from 2008 through to 2013, can be used. Paid-for editions of VisualStudio may work but not guaranteed.

Recent versions of Mingw may also be used.

Linux

CentOS 6 and Ubuntu 12-14 are compatible, in both 32-bit and 64-bit flavours.

C/C++ compiler toolchains

It is recommended a recent version of GCC is used, such as 4.8.

Mono

Most Linux distros do not come with a modern Mono build. Compiling it yourself is not difficult.

OSX

OSX 10.8-10.10 are compatible, in 64-bit Intel flavours.

C/C++ compiler toolchains

Xcode 4 through 6 can be used

Mono

Mono can be downloaded from the Mono project
